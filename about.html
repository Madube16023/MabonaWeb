About Us

Mabona Funeral Undertaker was established in 2002 in Orlando West/Mzimhlope uptown in South Africa. We serve our nation with pride and dignity. Mr Topollo Lancelot Mabona always had an eye and mind for business early in the years and thus saw the greatest opportunity of working with the community and that's when Mabona Funeral Undertaker was born. During that time, Mr Mabona was working with his wife, Mrs Kgomotso Mabona and his right-hand man, Mr Sipho Sibeko and together they escalated Mabona Funeral Undertaker to where it is today. Today, Mabona Funeral Undertaker has EMPLOYED more than 20 people who work tirelessly and contribute to the success of the company.
